Explore Our Project

People from the LGBT+ community are often outcasted from society and may find it difficult to interact normally with other people. Although the situation is improving, it's still not enough.

Still too many people raise walls between "us" and "them". They tend to infer others' sexual orientation and gender identity according to how they look and behave, and label them with specific beliefs and expectations. Basically, they believe not only that others belong to a category based on their appearance, but also that everybody in that category would have the same characteristics. .

Why does this happen? It's simple: they have never interacted with one of them. As human beings, we share more than we would like to think.

This project aims at providing means to fight these stereotypes as well as a supportive and positive environment in which people can engage with each other according to what they have in common.
