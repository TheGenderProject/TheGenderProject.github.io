Explore Our Project

Human sexuality is still treated as a taboo by too many people around the world. Finding reliable information may not an easy task and although research in gender and sex issues is becoming more and more popular, little has been done to apply that understand in every-day contexts.

This project aims at providing an environment in which experts on the field can work together to suggest innovative solutions, available for educational purposes to anyone who is interested.
